FREQUENTLY ASKED

Common Questions

How fast can you respond to a residential lockout in Los Angeles?

Our average response time in the Los Angeles metro area is under 20 minutes. We dispatch immediately on every call and keep you updated with an ETA from your technician.

Will you damage my door or lock when opening it?

In the vast majority of cases, we open the door without any damage using professional non-destructive entry tools. If drilling is necessary, we tell you before starting and get your approval first.

Should I rekey or replace my locks when I move into a new home?

Rekeying is almost always the right answer for a new home purchase. It's faster, less expensive, and achieves the same security result as replacement when the existing hardware is in good condition.

Do you charge more for after-hours emergency calls?

We have a straightforward pricing structure quoted over the phone before dispatch. After-hours rates are clearly stated upfront so there are no surprises on the invoice.

Is emergency service available throughout California?

Yes. Locksmith Luz emergency service is available throughout California, with the fastest response times in the Los Angeles metro area. Response times to other cities vary but we dispatch immediately.

Can you make a car key without the original?

In most cases, yes. Using your vehicle identification number and proof of ownership, we can cut and program a new key even when no original key exists.

Is using a locksmith cheaper than the dealership for a car key?

Typically yes, often significantly so. Locksmith Luz provides upfront pricing and comes to you, saving you a tow as well.

Can you design a master key system for my building?

Yes. We design master key hierarchies from scratch, accounting for tenant access levels, management access, and emergency access. We document the entire system for the building owner.

Do you service commercial properties outside of business hours?

Absolutely. Many clients prefer scheduling lock work during off-hours to avoid disrupting customers or employees. We accommodate early morning, evening, and weekend appointments.
